Dog House

In SketchUp we made dog houses following a video tutorial and then added onto it.

After we made the dog house we were instructed to find a dog in the "3D Warehouse" where you can find user created models to add to your project. I found a funny looking dog that was sitting down on it's legs like a toddler and holding up a concrete circle like it was using it as a weight so I put a scaled up fishing weight on it. I made a shelf for weights beside the dog house aswell.
